# 📰 NewsApp - Breaking News Delivery Game

A fast-paced interactive game where you deliver breaking news updates while navigating obstacles and managing your score.

## 🎮 Game Overview

Navigate the night sky, collect news items, avoid obstacles, and deliver breaking news. Test your reflexes and strategic gameplay to achieve the highest score!

## 📋 Features

- **Dynamic Gameplay**: Smooth physics-based movement with gravity mechanics
- **Score Tracking**: Earn points with each successful news delivery
- **Lives System**: Manage your health with a 3-life system
- **Invincibility Frames**: Brief protection after getting hit
- **Responsive Controls**: Intuitive keyboard controls for smooth gameplay
- **Space Theme**: Beautiful night sky backdrop with animated elements
- **Real-time Updates**: Live score and lives display

## 🚀 Getting Started

### Quick Start
1. Open `index.html` in your web browser
2. Press any key or click to start playing
3. Use arrow keys or WASD to control your character
4. Collect news items and avoid obstacles

### Browser Requirements
- Modern browser with HTML5 Canvas support
- JavaScript ES6+ support

## 🎮 How to Play

- **Move**: Arrow keys or WASD
- **Jump**: Spacebar (hold for higher jump)
- **Objective**: Collect news items and avoid obstacles
- **Lives**: You have 3 lives - lose them all and it's game over
- **Score**: Each successful action increases your score

- **JavaScript (ES6+)**: Core game engine
